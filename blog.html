Kerala

Kerala a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast,has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline.National parks like Eravikulam and Periyar are home to elephants,langur,monkeys and tiger.

Darjeeling

Darjeeling is a town in India's West Bengal state,in the Himalayan foothills. It's famed for the distinctive black tea growth on plantations that dot its surronding slopes.

Goa

Goa is a state in western India with coastlines stretching along the Arabian sea. Goa is known for fishing, beaches, ranging from popular stretches at Bala and Palolem.

Mysuru

Mysuru a city in India's Karnataka state, was the capital of the Kingdom of Mysore from 1399 to 1947. The place blends Hindu, Islamic, Gothic and Rajput styles. It's also a home for Devraja Market filled with spices, silk and sandalwood.

Delhi

Delhi India's capital territory, is a massive area in the country's north. Delhi stands the imposing Mughal-era Red Fort, a symbol of India, and the sprawling Jama Mashjid mosque, whose courtyard accomodates 2500 people.

Ladakh

Ladakh is a region administered by India as a union territory and constituting a part of the larger region of Kashmir, which has been the subject of dispute between India, Pakistan and China since 1947.

Mumbai

Mumbai is a densely populated city on India's west coast. On the Mumbai Harbour waterfront stands the iconic gateway of India stone arch, built by the British Raj in 1924. This city is also famous as the heart of the Bollywood film industry.

Agra

Agra is a city on the banks of the Yamuna river in the Indian state of Uttar Pradesh. Agra is the forth-most populous city in Uttar Pradesh and 24th in India. Agra is also famous of Taj Mahal a seventh wonder of the world.
